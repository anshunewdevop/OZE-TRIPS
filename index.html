Frequently Asked Questions

Which place should i start my spiritual journey from?

Begin your spiritual journey in Varanasi (Kashi), where the river Ganga flows, ancient temples stand, and the vibrant atmosphere offers profound experiences. It's a perfect introduction to India's spiritual heritage.

Which months are best to visit Varanasi?

The best months to visit Varanasi are October to March, when the weather is cooler and pleasant, ideal for exploring temples, ghats, and experiencing the city's vibrant festivals.

What is the minimum cost for weekend trip to Kashi?

A weekend trip to Kashi typically costs between ₹5,000 to ₹15,000, covering transportation, accommodation, meals, and local sightseeing, depending on your preferences and travel style.

What is the subscription model for trips?

The subscription model for trips offers members access to exclusive travel packages, discounts, and personalized itineraries for a monthly fee, enhancing convenience and affordability for frequent travelers seeking new experiences.

What will be the cost for staying in Kashi for a month?

The approximate cost for staying a month in Kashi ranges from ₹15,000 to ₹30,000, depending on accommodation type, meals, and personal expenses, offering options for various budgets.

How can I attend the famous DEV DIWALI of Kashi?

To attend Dev Diwali in Kashi, plan your visit in November, book accommodation in advance, and participate in the vibrant festivities along the Ganga river, enjoying the breathtaking illuminated ghats.

What services do you offer?

We offer personalized itineraries, guided tours, comfortable accommodations, local cuisine experiences, transportation arrangements, and cultural activities, ensuring a memorable and enriching travel experience for all our travelers.

What discounts do you provide?

We provide exclusive discounts, seasonal promotions, early bird offers, group packages, and loyalty rewards, ensuring our travelers enjoy exceptional value and memorable experiences during their journeys with us.

What type of trips do you offer?

We offer diverse trips, including spiritual journeys to sacred sites, adventure excursions for thrill-seekers, cultural explorations of local heritage, and wellness retreats focused on relaxation and rejuvenation for all travelers.

What does OZE stands for?

Oze Trips, or One Zero Eight Trips, emphasize lightweight travel, minimizing baggage for hassle-free journeys. We promote sustainable exploration by encouraging efficient packing, enhancing mobility, and enriching travel experiences.
